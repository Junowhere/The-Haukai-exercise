OUR STORY

A specialty of the Bay of Islands, a taste of Maori Kai that you can't experience anywhere else in the world. We offer both Maori and Pakeha kai options, taste that will melt your taste buds. This is a local and tourist attraction that draws people from all around the world. You're getting a world class experience, a rare cultural taste that you'll remember for a lifetime.
Come with family and friends or just by yourself, you'll experience the true taste of Haukai.
Since the late 20th century traditional Māori foods have been prepared and presented in new ways to suit the modern palette. New products have also been developed from ancient ingredients, ensuring Māori cuisine continues to be enjoyed by many people in New Zealand and internationally.
